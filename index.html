01 What We Want From You

So… what do you
actually need to do?

Great question! It's simpler than you think. Four things. One day. Solo or with a partner — let's break it down 👇

01

Think about the customer calls you're on every day. What problem would a voice AI agent solve beautifully? Pick that. Something real, something impactful. The rule? You should be proud to say "I built that." That's it.

02
🤖 Build the thing

A live, callable LiveHub AI voice agent with a real phone number

Fire up LiveHub, build your agent, give it a phone number. Judges will literally call it. Your agent needs to pick up, have a real conversation, and handle your scenario from hello to goodbye. Make it work — then make it shine.

03

🏗️ Basic prompt structure

### Identity
You are [name], a friendly voice agent
for [company] [department].

### Context
[Background about the product/service]

### Objective
- [Task 1] / - [Task 2]

### Instructions
1. Ask caller for their name.
2. Determine their intent.
3. Use the get_slots tool.

### Format
Keep answers short.
Never use HTML or markdown.
You communicate through speech.

🎤 Voice-specific best practices

1. Always declare voice mode:
"You communicate through speech.
Avoid long responses, never use
HTML or markdown."

2. Use numbered steps:
"Follow steps in order. Ask up to
one question per response."

3. Be explicit about tools:
"Use get_appointments with
date set to today's date."

4. For branching:
"If caller says yes, go to step 3.
If no, end the call politely."
